Our Story


MILU's Fabrics is a local retail store and export organization based in Nepal. We provide a wide variety of hand crafted textiles and garments around the world. we blend traditional craft with modern design and technology to suit market needs and trends. We welcome buyers with creative unique ideas to customise an item for a completely new original product. We are a small scale company mainly focusing on Nepalese women empowerment.

Return Policy

We offer a 100% money back guarantee. You must advise us by email within three days of receipt that you plan to return the item. Then you must return it within 7 days after letting us know. All items must be returned in the same condition as they were shipped (no washing, pressing, sizing, cutting etc.). Original shipping charges are not refundable.

Shipping

Most orders are sent via Priority Mail with tracking at flat rates. If you need special shipping such as overnight for a special gift,insurance or delivery to another address, please specify that in your original order.
